Our Story

Where Every
Meal Becomes
A Memory

Perched 22 floors above the bustling streets of Hyderabad, LUMINA SKYDINING was born from a singular vision — to create a dining destination that transcends the ordinary.

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Arjun Mehta, blends the finest global techniques with the rich heritage of Indian flavours, crafting dishes that tell a story with every bite.

From the moment you step into our rooftop haven, the city skyline becomes your backdrop, the stars your ceiling, and the food — a journey you will savour long after the last course.

The Artist

Executive Chef

Arjun Mehta

"Cooking is not just a skill — it is a conversation between the chef and the soul of the ingredient."

With over 18 years of culinary mastery spanning Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Chef Arjun returned to his roots in Hyderabad to create something truly extraordinary — a menu that honours tradition while fearlessly embracing innovation.

His signature style — elegant minimalism with explosive flavour — has earned LUMINA a place among India's top 10 fine dining restaurants for three consecutive years.
